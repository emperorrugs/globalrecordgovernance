## Overview

GRGF includes a comprehensive capacity-building framework designed to prepare institutional staff for governance record infrastructure adoption.

## Role-Based Training Modules

### Ministry Staff & Decision-Makers
- Understanding governance records and their implications
- Authority context and delegation responsibilities
- Event reporting and omission recognition
- Privacy and data protection obligations

### Registry Operators
- Event normalization procedures
- System integration and data quality
- Record lifecycle management
- Error correction and supersession protocols

### Auditors & Oversight Officials
- Verification workflow execution
- Inclusion and consistency proof validation
- Checkpoint and witness signature verification
- Audit reconstruction techniques

### IT & Infrastructure Teams
- Deployment and configuration management
- Security hardening and monitoring
- Federation protocol setup
- Performance optimization and scaling

## Training Delivery

| Format | Duration | Audience |
|--------|----------|----------|
| Executive Briefing | 2 hours | Senior leadership |
| Operational Workshop | 2 days | Ministry staff |
| Technical Deep-Dive | 3 days | IT teams |
| Audit Certification | 5 days | Auditors |
| Simulation Exercise | 1 day | All roles |

## Certification Tiers

| Tier | Requirement | Scope |
|------|------------|-------|
| GRGF Aware | Briefing completion | Conceptual understanding |
| GRGF Operator | Workshop + assessment | Operational proficiency |
| GRGF Auditor | Deep-dive + examination | Verification competence |
| GRGF Architect | Full program + practicum | Deployment capability |

## Simulation Exercises

All training includes hands-on simulation using the GRGF demonstration environment:
- Governance event creation and recording
- Verification proof generation and validation
- Audit reconstruction from sealed records
- Federation checkpoint witnessing
